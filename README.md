# Cabinet

Cabinet is your personal vault to save, find, and copy your important data.

## Features
- Save snippets, passwords, codes, links, and any small pieces of important information.
- Fast search to find items instantly by title, tags, or content.
- One-tap copy to clipboard with haptic feedback.
- Organize with folders and tags (smart filters supported).
- iCloud sync to keep your data up to date across devices.
- Privacy-first: on-device encryption and no analytics by default.
- Widgets and Share Sheet extension for quick capture and access.
- Dark Mode and accessibility-friendly UI.

## Getting Started
- Requirements:
  - iOS 17 or later (update as appropriate for your app)
  - Xcode 15 or later
- Build & Run:
  1. Open the project in Xcode.
  2. Select the Cabinet app target.
  3. Press Run (⌘R) to build and launch on a device or simulator.

## Usage
- Create a new item: Tap the + button and enter a title, content, and optional tags.
- Search: Pull down in the list or use the search bar to filter by text or tags.
- Copy: Tap an item to view details; use the Copy button to place content on the clipboard.
- Organize: Drag items into folders or add tags for smart filtering.
- Share Extension: From any app, choose Share → Cabinet to save content quickly.

## Privacy & Security
- Data is stored securely on-device and synced via iCloud when enabled.
- Sensitive fields can be masked by default and revealed on demand.
- We do not collect personal analytics by default. Opt-in diagnostics may be available.
